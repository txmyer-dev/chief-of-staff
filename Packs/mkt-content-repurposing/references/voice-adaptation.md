# Voice Adaptation

Your brand voice doesn't change per platform — the delivery does. Same person, different room. You wouldn't talk to a LinkedIn audience the way you'd talk on TikTok, but you'd still be you.

This reference covers how to shift delivery without losing identity.

---

## How Each Platform Wants You to Show Up

| Platform | Register | Pace | How Long | What They Want |
|----------|----------|------|----------|----------------|
| LinkedIn | Polished but not corporate | Measured | 1,200-1,500 chars | Proof you know your stuff |
| Twitter/X | Sharp, no padding | Fast | Under 280 chars | Say it quick or thread it |
| Instagram | Warm, visual-first | Relaxed | Caption-length | Make them feel something |
| TikTok | Unfiltered, high-energy | Rapid | 15-60 sec spoken | Be real or get scrolled |
| YouTube | Like talking to a mate | Natural | Script-length | Depth with personality |
| Threads | Chatty, thinking out loud | Easy | 500 chars max | Join the conversation |
| Bluesky | Considered, specific | Calm | 300 chars max | Substance over sizzle |
| Reddit | Transparent, detailed | Unhurried | As long as needed | Earn trust with specifics |

## Rules for Adapting

1. **Shift the register, keep the personality.** If the brand is playful, it stays playful everywhere — just more restrained on LinkedIn, more loose on TikTok.
2. **Change the vocabulary.** LinkedIn: "framework", "methodology." TikTok: "literally", "the thing is." Reddit: "FWIW", "IME." Match how people actually talk on each platform.
3. **Lead differently.** LinkedIn opens with credibility. Twitter opens with the punchline. TikTok opens with a pattern interrupt. Reddit opens with "here's who I am and why you should listen."
4. **Match the CTA energy.** LinkedIn: "Repost if useful." Twitter: "RT the first tweet." TikTok: "Part 2?" Reddit: "Happy to answer questions in the comments."
5. **Respect the length.** A LinkedIn-length piece doesn't belong on Twitter. A tweet-length take doesn't belong on Reddit. Write to the platform, not to your word count.

## Weekly Content Rhythm (5-3-2)

For calendar mode. Spread the repurposed content across a week:

- **5 value posts:** Carousels, threads, tutorials. The meat from your source content.
- **3 engagement posts:** Questions, polls, spicy takes. Get people talking.
- **2 personal posts:** Stories, behind-the-scenes, real talk. Build connection.

## CTAs That Fit Each Platform

**LinkedIn:** "Repost if someone in your network needs this" / "Save this one" / "Follow for more [topic]"

**Twitter/X:** "RT the first tweet" / "Follow for more" / "Bookmark this"

**Instagram:** "Save this" / "Send to someone who needs it" / "Drop a [word] in comments for [thing]"

**TikTok:** "Follow for part 2" / "Save this" / "Link in bio"

**YouTube:** "Subscribe + bell" / "Watch this next" / "Tell me in the comments"

**Threads:** "What's your take?" / "Repost if this landed" / "Quote with your version"

**Bluesky:** "What's been your experience?" / "Add this to your [topic] feed"

**Reddit:** "Happy to answer questions below" / "If this was useful I'll do a Part 2 on [topic]"
