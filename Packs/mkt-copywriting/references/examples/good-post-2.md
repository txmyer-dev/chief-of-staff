# Example: LinkedIn Post (Contrarian AI Tools Take)

---

Stop collecting AI tools. You don't need 14 of them.

I counted the tabs open on a client's browser last week. 14 AI tools. Subscriptions totalling over $400/month.

And they were still doing most of the work manually.

Here's the actual problem: more tools doesn't mean less work. It usually means more context-switching, more logins, more "let me just check this other thing first."

What actually works is the opposite:

1. Pick one tool that handles your biggest time drain
2. Set it up properly with your voice and your context
3. Let it run for 30 days before even thinking about adding another

The people I see getting real results aren't tool collectors. They're system builders. One tool, configured well, running on autopilot.

But here's what nobody talks about: the setup matters more than the tool. A perfectly configured free tool beats a badly configured $99/month tool every single time.

So before you sign up for AI tool number 15, ask yourself: have you actually finished setting up the first one?

What's the one AI tool you'd keep if you could only pick one?
