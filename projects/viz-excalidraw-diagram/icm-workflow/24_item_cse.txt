24-ITEM STORY EXPLANATION:
1. Prospect enters the Sales Floor (ICM CRM) looking for business transformation.
2. The sales agent initiates the Onboarding process.
3. Client details are captured in intake.yaml (The Blueprint).
4. CRM profile.yaml is initialized to track the relationship.
5. A service Package is selected (e.g., Web Dev, Social Media).
6. Pre-flight checks begin to ensure project feasibility.
7. Project Management silo generates the initial Contract and Project Plan.
8. Client signs the contract (The Green Light).
9. ICM-Activate is triggered to start the production engine.
10. The Artifact Bus is seeded with initial data from intake.yaml.
11. Initial artifacts (Business Goals, Brand Values) land on the conveyor belt.
12. The Factory (ICM Tree) comes alive as silos scan the Artifact Bus.
13. Product Silo sees Business Goals and starts Discovery.
14. Silos that lack inputs remain in 'Blocked' state (Waiting at the station).
15. Product Silo finishes Discovery and writes a Research Report to its output/ folder.
16. ICM-Complete is run, promoting the Research Report to the Artifact Bus.
17. The Research Report moves along the conveyor belt to the next silos.
18. UI Silo sees the Research Report and unblocks to start the Design System.
19. Engineering Silo waits for the Design System and Technical Specs.
20. Multiple silos work in parallel as the Artifact Bus fulfills their needs.
21. Final deliverables (Source Code, QA Cert) are promoted to the Bus.
22. The finished 'Product' is transported back to the Sales Floor.
23. The sales agent presents the final work to the happy Client.
24. CRM record is updated to 'Success' and the factory prepares for the next run.

STRUCTURAL ELEMENTS IDENTIFIED:
- Processes: Onboarding, Pre-flight, Activation, Execution, Promotion, Delivery.
- Decisions: Contract approval, Input readiness checks (icm-activate).
- States: Prospect, Onboarded, Active, Blocked, Complete, Delivered.
- Entities: ICM CRM (Sales Floor), Artifact Bus (Conveyor), ICM Tree (Factory), Client, Silos.
- Flows: Unidirectional flow from CRM → Bus → Factory (initiation); Bidirectional flow during execution (promotion); Return flow from Factory → Bus → CRM (delivery).
- Conditions: Input satisfying dependencies unblocks silos.
